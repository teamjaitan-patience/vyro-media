### BRAND VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

**Voice Personality:**
VYRO speaks like a creative director who grew up on Bollywood but studied Silicon Valley: cinematic in soul, sharp in strategy. Never corporate. Never cold. Always with a pulse.

**Tone Pillars:**
1. **Cinematic:** Every sentence has weight. We write like we shoot.
2. **Honest:** No fluff. No buzzwords. We say what we do and do what we say.
3. **Ambitious:** We speak to the version of you that's becoming something.
4. **Human:** Behind every brand is a story. We remember that.

**What VYRO Sounds Like:**
* Warm but not mushy
* Confident but not arrogant
* Technical but never boring
* Storytelling-first, results-second (because good stories *create* results)

**What VYRO Never Sounds Like:**
* "Disruptive synergies"
* "End-to-end solutions"
* "We help brands scale"
* Anything that sounds like it was written by a committee

### SECTION 12: FAQ

**Q: I'm based outside Delhi. Can we still work together?**
A: Absolutely. Most of our content strategy, editing, and website work happens remotely. For production shoots in Delhi-NCR, we're on the ground. For other cities, we can discuss.

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
A: Websites: 7–18 days. Video edits: 3–7 days. Full production shoots: depends on scope. We always give a clear timeline upfront. No ghosting, no surprises.

**Q: Do you work with very early-stage founders or small budgets?**
A: Yes. Our website starter package starts at ₹15,000 and we build content strategies for creators at every stage. We'd rather work with someone ambitious at ₹15k than someone indifferent at ₹1.5L.

**Q: What makes VYRO different from other agencies?**
A: Most agencies scale by hiring more people and building templates. We scale by being deeply involved and using AI workflows to deliver more without losing quality. Every project has the founder's eye on it.
